Project Overview

The LanDAT project provides a monitoring tool for assessing whether ongoing landscape change is leading to desirable outcomes, and for understanding landscape resilience in a quantitative and integrative way.

This map viewer gives users the ability to explore data layers and interact with information updated on a regular basis to reach informed interpretations and decisions regarding landscape dynamics.

Map Layers

The Layers section allows you to view spatial landscape information on the map — both datasets created by the LanDAT project and ancillary layers from other data providers.

  • To put a layer on the map click on the checkbox next to its label.
  • To expand or collapse a group of related layers click on the group's bar.
  • To view a detailed description of a layer click on the button for that layer.
  • To adjust the transparency of a layer move the slider marked with a icon.

Graphs

The Vegetation Index Graphs section visualizes the change over time in vegetation greenness — the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) — at locations you specify.

  • To bring up a graph for a location click on that location on the map.
  • To zoom the map to a graphs location click on the "Show On Map" button.
  • To scroll the list of graphs to an existing location's plot click on the corresponding marker — — on the map.
  • To remove a graph from the list — and its marker on the map — click on the button for that graph.
