The LanDAT Map Viewer gives users the ability to explore data layers and interact with information updated on a regular basis to reach informed interpretations and decisions regarding landscape dynamics.

Click on the map to mark a point of interest. The Vegetation Index Graphs section visualizes the change over time in vegetation greenness — the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) — of the latitude/longitude at each point of interest. Several graph types are available. Each graph has a "Show On Map" button to track its corresponding map marker. You can also click on a marker to be taken directly to its NDVI graph.
